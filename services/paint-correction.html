What's Included

Paint correction goes beyond a simple buff & polish. Using multi-stage machine polishing with progressively finer compounds, we systematically remove deeper defects that regular polishing can't touch. The result is a flawless, mirror-like finish that restores your vehicle's true colour depth and clarity.

  • Full exterior wash & thorough decontamination
  • Iron fallout removal & clay bar treatment
  • Paint depth gauge measurement for safe correction
  • Multi-stage machine polishing (up to 3 stages)
  • Removal of moderate to heavy swirl marks
  • Scratch removal & oxidation treatment
  • Water spot & etch mark correction
  • Final refinement for mirror-gloss finish
  • IPA wipe-down and inspection under LED lights

Why Choose This Service
