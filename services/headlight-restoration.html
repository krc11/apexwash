Headlight Restoration

What's Included

Over time, headlight lenses become cloudy, yellowed, and oxidised — dramatically reducing visibility at night and making your car look older than it is. Our headlight restoration service uses wet sanding and machine polishing to remove the damaged outer layer, then seals it with a UV-protective coating to keep them crystal clear for longer.

  • Masking & protection of surrounding paint
  • Multi-grit wet sanding to remove oxidation
  • Machine polishing for optical clarity
  • Removal of yellowing & hazing
  • UV sealant application for lasting protection
  • Both headlights included in the service
