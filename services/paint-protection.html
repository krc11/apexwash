What's Included

Our paint protection service uses advanced ceramic coating technology to create a durable, hydrophobic layer over your vehicle's paintwork. This invisible shield defends against UV damage, bird droppings, tree sap, road grime, and harsh Australian weather — keeping your car cleaner for longer with dramatically less maintenance.

  • Full exterior wash & chemical decontamination
  • Clay bar treatment to remove bonded contaminants
  • Light machine polish to prepare surface (if needed)
  • Panel prep / IPA wipe-down for perfect bonding
  • Professional-grade ceramic coating application
  • Choice of 1-year or 5-year coating durability
  • Hydrophobic finish — water beads and sheets off
  • Enhanced UV protection against fading & oxidation
